How I work

Three principles for financial product management.

Compliance is a feature

I don't treat Compliance/Legal as a gate at the end. I partner with them from discovery — identifying disclosure requirements, suitability logic, and audit needs before the first PRD. 93% audit pass rates don't happen by accident.

Data models before dashboards

Advisors don't trust tools that show different numbers than Morningstar. I obsess over foundational data alignment — share classes, vehicle types, calculation logic — before building a single view. That's how recon errors go from 12% to 1.5%.

Time-to-value is the metric

An advisor won't use a tool slower than their spreadsheet. Every workflow I ship — proposal, review, due diligence — is measured by minutes saved versus the manual process. If it's not faster, it doesn't ship.
